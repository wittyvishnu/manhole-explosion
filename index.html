Introduction

Manhole explosions are dangerous occurrences that can cause serious damage to infrastructure and pose a threat to public safety. These explosions, often caused by a buildup of combustible gases, can result in property damage, injuries, and even fatalities. Understanding the causes, working, impact, and prevention of manhole explosions is crucial for ensuring the safety of urban environments. In this presentation, we will explore the intricacies of manhole explosions, their impact, and how IoT systems can be utilized to overcome these hazards.


Currently, numerous nations are prioritizing the development of underground drainage systems as a key component of urban infrastructure. So whenever there is more a concentration of methane which leads to explosion, it is difficult to figure out the exact location of the place. Also, early alerts of the manhole explosion are not received. Hence detection and preventation become more difficult. It becomes very inconvenient to handle the situation when explosion as taken place. Due to such explosion people face a lot of problems.

Working of IoT in Manhole Explosion

Ardunio Uno

An open-source microcontroller platform that can be used to build various IoT devices for monitoring and managing manhole conditions

LM35 Sensor

A component capable of measuring the temperature within manholes, providing crucial data for detecting hazardous conditions.

MQ4 Gas Sensor

This sensor is designed to detect the presence of flammable and explosive gases, enabling proactive measures to prevent explosions.

GSM SIM Module

Enables real-time communication and data transmission from IoT devices installed in manholes to a central monitoring system.

Water Level Sensor

Monitors water accumulation in manholes to prevent electrical malfunctions and identify potential gas displacement risks.

GPS

Provides location-based data to track manhole conditions and facilitate rapid response to any impending explosion threats.

APPLICATIONS AND ADVANTAGES OF WORK

Real-time Alerts

In the event of abnormal conditions or emergencies, IOT systems can send realtime alerts to relevant authorities or maintenance teams, enabling a quick response to mitigate potential risks.

Early Detection

IOT sensors enable early detection of issues or abnormalities, allowing for proactive measures to prevent disasters or accidents

Data-Driven Decision Making

The data collected by IOT devices can be analyzed to make informed decisions regarding maintenance schedules, resource allocation, and infrastructure improvements.

Cost Savings

The financial cost of repairing infrastructure damage caused by manhole explosions can be significant. An IoT system that prevents these explosions can result in cost savings for utility companies and municipalities by reducing the need for repairs and maintenance.
